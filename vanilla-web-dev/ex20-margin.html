margin

依次是：未指定 margin、margin: 0 5px;、margin: 0 -5px;

当 margin 值为负值时，含义就不是远离，而是靠近、嵌入。

auto margin

常用的 auto 有两种，一种是 Block layout mode，当左右均 auto 时，居中放置；一种是 Flexbox layout mode，左右、上下的 auto 均可生效，只要有空余空间


margin collapsing

只针对 top, bottom margin，不会影响 left, right margin

Vertical margins on different elements that touch each other (thus have no content, padding, or borders separating them) will collapse, forming a single margin that is equal to the greater of the adjoining margins. This does not happen on horizontal margins (left and right), only vertical (top and bottom). https://css-tricks.com/almanac/properties/m/margin/

The top and bottom margins of blocks are sometimes combined (collapsed) into a single margin whose size is the largest of the individual margins (or just one of them, if they are equal), a behavior known as margin collapsing.
